## Registry Notes

- `verified` means official product/spec documentation verifies the core screen or mechanical fact, with media used only to fill context.
- `media-reported` means the product exists and facts are supported by reputable media, review, or crowdfunding material, but no complete official spec page was found for the relevant research facts.
- `unknown-after-official-and-media-search` is reserved for individual facts that remained not confirmed after checking official and media sources.
- `not-applicable` is used when the product category lacks that component by design, such as DGX Spark having no built-in display.
- Higole correction: `public/images/competitors/local/higole-scene.png` is not used as final Higole evidence. The corrected Higole/Gole1 Pro image reference is the CNX Software product image listed above, cross-checked against the Gole retail page.
